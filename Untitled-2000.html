The iBuildSG Tripartite Committee (TC) was set up in Feb. 2019 by Building
and Construction Authority (BCA) in Singapore to foster tripartite collabo-
ration and efforts among IHLs, TACs, Professional Boards and BCA, across
the disciplines in the Built and Environment (BE) sector, such as Architec-
ture, Civil Engineering, Facility Management, etc. Following the initiative,
The International Building Design Competition 2020 was organized by
Singapore Building & Construction Authority in partnership with SP, NUS,
NTU, SUTD.
The theme for this year’s competition is “Sustainable & Intelligent City”,
stressing on the importance of designing sustainable and smart buildings
in urban Singapore. The competition was to design a mixed-use develop-
ment integrating residential, commercial, retail, commercial, community
and transportation usages under one roof, and required participants to
demonstrate their knowledge in sustainable building design, Design for
Manufacturing and Assembly, as well as Integrated Digital Delivery.
