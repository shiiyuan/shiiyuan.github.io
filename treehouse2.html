TREEHOUSE

The iBuildSG Tripartite Committee (TC) was set up in Feb. 2019 by Building and Con-struction Authority (BCA) in Singapore to foster tripartite collaboration and efforts among IHLs, TACs, Professional Boards and BCA, across the disciplines in the Built and Environment (BE) sector, such as Architecture, Civil Engineering, Facility Man-agement, etc. Following the initiative, The International Building Design Competi-tion 2020 was organized by Singapore Building & Construction Authority in partner-ship with SP, NUS, NTU, SUTD.

The theme for this year’s competition is “Sustainable & Intelligent City”, stressing on the importance of designing sustainable and smart buildings in urban Singa-pore. The competition was to design a mixed-use development integrating residen-tial, commercial, retail, commercial, community and transportation usages under one roof, and required participants to demonstrate their knowledge in sustainable building design, Design for Manufacturing and Assembly, as well as Integrated Digi-tal Delivery.
